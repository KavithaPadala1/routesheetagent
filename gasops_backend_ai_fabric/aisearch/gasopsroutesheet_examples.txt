
User : show me the breakdown of tickets by work desc in gas ops routesheet
SQL:
SELECT
  td.WorkDescription,
  COUNT(td.TicketNumber) AS TicketCount
FROM cedemo_RouteSheetHeader h
JOIN cedemo_RouteSheetTicketDetails td ON h.RouteSheetID = td.RouteSheetID
WHERE h.IsActive = 1
  AND td.IsActive = 1
  AND YEAR(h.RouteSheetDate) = 2026
GROUP BY td.WorkDescription
ORDER BY TicketCount DESC;

User : For the last 30 days, show me a breakdown of tickets in the bronx as a function of job description
SQL:
SELECT td.WorkDescription, COUNT(td.TicketNumber) AS TicketCount
FROM vm_cedemo_routesheetheader h
JOIN cedemo_RouteSheetTicketDetails td ON h.RouteSheetID = td.RouteSheetID
WHERE h.IsActive = 1
  AND td.IsActive = 1
  AND h.Region = 'X'
  AND CAST(h.RouteSheetDate AS DATE) >= DATEADD(DAY,-30,'2026-03-23')
GROUP BY td.WorkDescription
ORDER BY TicketCount DESC;
