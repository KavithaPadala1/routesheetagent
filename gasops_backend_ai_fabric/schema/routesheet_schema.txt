## TYPE: Gas Operations Route Sheet

TABLE : cedemo_RouteSheetHeader
Description: Stores header-level information for each route sheet, including scheduling details, assigned personnel, contact information, and status tracking.
Columns:
- RouteSheetID (bigint): Unique identifier for the route sheet record.
- RouteSheetDate (datetime2): Date and time associated with the route sheet.
- RouteSheetDay (varchar): Day of the week for the route sheet.It can have Mon,Monday,Tue,Tuesday,Wed,Wednesday,Thu,Thursday,Fri,Friday,Sat,Saturday,Sun,Sunday
- Shift (varchar): Shift designation for the route sheet. It can have Night,Day,Midnight
- Region (varchar): Region associated with the route sheet. It can have 'X' , 'M' , 'Q' , and 'W'.
- District (varchar): District within the region for the route sheet.
- SectionManagerID (bigint): Identifier of the section manager assigned to the route sheet.
- ManagerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the section manager.
- FieldOperationsPlannerID (bigint): Identifier of the field operations planner assigned to the route sheet.
- PlannerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the field operations planner.
- OperatingSupervisorID (bigint): Identifier of the operating supervisor assigned to the route sheet.
- SupervisorPhone (varchar): Phone number of the operating supervisor.
- Gerc (varchar):
- EHSHotline (varchar):
- RouteSheetStatus (varchar): Current status of the route sheet.It can have Submitted, NULL
- IsActive (bit): Indicates whether the route sheet record is active. Always consider IsActive=1
- CreatedBy (int): Identifier of the user who created the record.
- CreatedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was created.
- ModifiedBy (int): Identifier of the user who last modified the record.
- ModifiedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was last modified.
- AdditonalNotes (varchar): Additional Notes

TABLE : cedemo_RouteSheetTicketDetails
Description: Stores ticket-level details associated with a route sheet, including work order information, inspection assignments, second inspector tracking, and operational flags.
Columns:
- RouteSheetTicketDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the ticket details record.
- RouteSheetID (bigint): Foreign key referencing the associated route sheet header.
- TicketNumber (varchar): Ticket number associated with the work item.
- WorkOrderID (bigint): Identifier of the related work order.
- WorkLocation (varchar): Location where the work is to be performed.
- RouteSheetWorkDescriptionID (bigint): Identifier referencing the work description lookup.
- WorkDescription (varchar): Description of the work to be performed.
- SecondInspector (varchar): Name or identifier of the second inspector assigned.
- ConditionID (varchar):
- Muni (varchar): Municipality associated with the ticket.
- Markout (varchar): Indicates whether a markout is required.
- Flagger (varchar): Indicates whether a flagger is required.
- FusingReq (varchar): Indicates whether fusing is required.
- IsActive (bit): Indicates whether the ticket details record is active.
- CreatedBy (int): Identifier of the user who created the record.
- CreatedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was created.
- ModifiedBy (int): Identifier of the user who last modified the record.
- ModifiedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was last modified.
- EmergencyPermit (varchar): Indicates whether an emergency permit is associated with the ticket.
- FlaggerQuantity (varchar): Number of flaggers required for the work.
- MarkoutDescription (varchar): Description of the markout requirements.
- ClericalNote (varchar): Administrative or clerical notes associated with the ticket.
- FusingPeerNeedCount (varchar): Number of fusing peers needed.
- AssignedSecondInspectorVendorCode (varchar): Vendor code of the assigned second inspector.
- SecondInspectorAssignedByCrew (varchar): Crew that assigned the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorAssignedByID (varchar): ID of the person who assigned the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorAssignedByName (varchar): Name of the person who assigned the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorCrew (varchar): Crew the second inspector belongs to.
- SecondInspectorVendorCode (varchar): Vendor code of the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorEmployeeMasterID (varchar): Employee master ID of the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorNameITSID (varchar): ITS ID of the second inspector.
- SecondInspectorAddUpdateByID (varchar): ID of the user who added or last updated the second inspector assignment.
- SecondInspectorAddUpdateByName (varchar): Name of the user who added or last updated the second inspector assignment.
- SecondInspectorQualified (varchar): Indicates whether the second inspector is qualified.
- SecondInspectorDISAPool (varchar):
- SecondInspectorNotes (varchar): Notes related to the second inspector assignment.
- SecondInspectorCancelled (bit): Indicates whether the second inspector assignment was cancelled.
- SecondInspectorCancelledDate (datetime2): Date and time when the second inspector assignment was cancelled.

TABLE : cedemo_RSAssignedEmployeeDetails
Description: Stores details of employees assigned to route sheet tickets, including identification, role, qualification status, and equipment information.
Columns:
- RSAssignedEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the assigned employee details record.
- RouteSheetTicketDetailsID (bigint): Foreign key referencing the associated route sheet ticket details record.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS system ID of the assigned employee.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar):
- EmployeeName (varchar): Full name of the assigned employee.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identification number.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar):
- JobTitle (varchar): Job title of the assigned employee.
- TruckNumber (varchar): Truck number assigned to the employee for this ticket.
- PhoneRadioNumber (varchar): Phone or radio number used to contact the employee.
- RouteSheetWorkDescriptionID (bigint): Identifier referencing the work description assigned to the employee.
- WorkDescription (varchar): Description of the work assigned to the employee.
- Qualified (varchar): Indicates whether the employee is qualified for the assigned work.
- QualificationValidatedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the employee's qualification was validated.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the assigned employee record has been soft-deleted.
- CreatedBy (int): Identifier of the user who created the record.
- CreatedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was created.
- ModifiedBy (int): Identifier of the user who last modified the record.
- ModifiedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was last modified.
- EmployeeDISAPool (varchar):
- MissingCoveredTasks (varchar): Lists required covered tasks that the employee has not completed. Multiple tasks may be stored as semicolon-separated values.

TABLE : cedemo_RSLeaveEmployeeDetails
Description: Stores details of employees on leave associated with a route sheet, including identification and reason for absence.
Columns:
- RSLeaveEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the leave employee details record.
- RouteSheetID (bigint): Foreign key referencing the associated route sheet header.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS system ID of the employee on leave.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar):
- EmployeeName (varchar): Full name of the employee on leave.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identification number.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar):
- ReasonForAbsence (varchar): Reason for the employee's absence.
- Comments (varchar): Additional comments regarding the employee's leave.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the leave record has been soft-deleted. ALways consider IsDeleted=0
- CreatedBy (int): Identifier of the user who created the record.
- CreatedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was created.
- ModifiedBy (int): Identifier of the user who last modified the record.
- ModifiedDate (datetime2): Date and time when the record was last modified.
