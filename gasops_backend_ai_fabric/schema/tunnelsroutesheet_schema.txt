Tunnels Routesheet Schema :

# TABLE : cedemo_TunnelsRouteSheetHeader  
Description:  
Stores header-level information for tunnels route sheets, including route scheduling details, supervisors, management roles, contact information, and overall route sheet status.

## Columns:
- TunnelsRouteSheetID (bigint): Unique identifier for the tunnels route sheet record.
- RouteSheetDate (datetime2): Date and time associated with the route sheet.
- RouteSheetDay (varchar): Day of the route sheet.
- ShiftData (varchar): Shift-related information for the route sheet.
- Supervisor1ID (bigint): Identifier of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Name (varchar): Name of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Phone (varchar): Phone number of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor2ID (bigint): Identifier of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Name (varchar): Name of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Phone (varchar): Phone number of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor3ID (bigint): Identifier of the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Name (varchar): Name of the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Phone (varchar): Phone number of the third supervisor.
- EHSSpecialistID (bigint): Identifier of the EHS specialist.
- EHSSpecialistName (varchar): Name of the EHS specialist.
- EHSSpecialistVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the EHS specialist.
- EHSSpecialistPhone (varchar): Phone number of the EHS specialist.
- ManagerID (bigint): Identifier of the manager.
- ManagerName (varchar): Name of the manager.
- ManagerVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the manager.
- ManagerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the manager.
- PlannerID (bigint): Identifier of the planner.
- PlannerName (varchar): Name of the planner.
- PlannerVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the planner.
- PlannerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the planner.
- EngineerID (bigint): Identifier of the engineer.
- EngineerName (varchar): Name of the engineer.
- EngineerVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the engineer.
- EngineerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the engineer.
- ProjectSpecialistID (bigint): Identifier of the project specialist.
- ProjectSpecialistName (varchar): Name of the project specialist.
- ProjectSpecialistVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the project specialist.
- ProjectSpecialistPhone (varchar): Phone number of the project specialist.
- RouteSheetStatus (varchar): Current status of the route sheet.
- AdditonalNotes (varchar): Additional notes related to the route sheet.
- IsActive (bit): Indicates whether the record is active. Always apply IsActive = 1.


# TABLE : cedemo_TunnelsRSAssignedEmployeeDetails  
Description:  
Stores employee-level assignment details for tunnels route sheets, including work location, supervisor mapping, employee identification, qualifications, and work descriptions.

## Columns:
- TunnelsRSAssignedEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the assigned employee details record.
- TunnelsRouteSheetID (bigint): Identifier linking the employee to a tunnels route sheet.
- WorkLocation (varchar): Location where the employee is assigned to work.
- SupervisorID (bigint): Identifier of the supervisor overseeing the employee.
- SupervisorName (varchar): Name of the supervisor.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS identifier of the employee.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar): CECT ITS identifier of the employee.
- EmployeeName (varchar): Full name of the employee.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identifier.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar): Master employee identifier in CECT system.
- EmployeeVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the employee.
- AccountNumber (varchar): Account number associated with the employee.
- WorkDescriptionID (bigint): Identifier of the work description.
- WorkDescription (varchar): Description of the work assigned.
- EmployeeDISAPool (varchar): Indicates whether the employee belongs to the DISA pool.
- Qualified (varchar): Indicates whether the employee is qualified. Has Yes, No values.
- MissingCoveredTasks (varchar): List of tasks not covered by the employee qualifications.
- QualificationValidatedDate (datetime2): Date when qualification was validated.
- Notes (varchar): Additional notes related to the employee assignment.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the record is deleted. Always consider IsDeleted = 0.


# TABLE : cedemo_TunnelsRSLeaveEmployeeDetails  
Description:  
Stores details of employees who are on leave for tunnels route sheets, including absence reasons, employee identification, and audit tracking information.

## Columns:
- TunnelsRSLeaveEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the leave employee record.
- TunnelsRouteSheetID (bigint): Identifier linking the leave record to a tunnels route sheet.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS identifier of the employee.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar): CECT ITS identifier of the employee.
- EmployeeName (varchar): Name of the employee on leave.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identifier.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar): Master employee identifier in CECT system.
- ReasonForAbsence (varchar): Reason for the employee’s absence.
- Comments (varchar): Additional comments related to the absence.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the record is deleted. Always consider IsDeleted = 0.


RELATIONSHIPS:
cedemo_TunnelsRouteSheetHeader.TunnelsRouteSheetID = cedemo_TunnelsRSAssignedEmployeeDetails.TunnelsRouteSheetID
cedemo_TunnelsRouteSheetHeader.TunnelsRouteSheetID = cedemo_TunnelsRSLeaveEmployeeDetails.TunnelsRouteSheetID


FILTER CONDITIONS (ALWAYS APPLY):
- cedemo_TunnelsRouteSheetHeader.IsActive = 1
- cedemo_TunnelsRSAssignedEmployeeDetails.IsDeleted = 0
- cedemo_TunnelsRSLeaveEmployeeDetails.IsDeleted = 0
