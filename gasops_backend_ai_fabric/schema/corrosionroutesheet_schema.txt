Corrossion Routesheet Schema :

# TABLE : cedemo_CorrosionRouteSheetHeader  
Description:  
Stores header-level information for corrosion route sheets, including supervisors, planner, section manager, contact details, and overall route sheet status.

## Columns:
- CorrosionRouteSheetID (bigint): Unique identifier for the corrosion route sheet record.
- RouteSheetDate (datetime2): Date and time associated with the route sheet.
- RouteSheetDay (varchar): Day of the route sheet.
- Supervisor1ID (bigint): Identifier of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Name (varchar): Name of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the first supervisor.
- Supervisor1Phone (varchar): Phone number of the first supervisor.
- Supervisor2ID (bigint): Identifier of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Name (varchar): Name of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the second supervisor.
- Supervisor2Phone (varchar): Phone number of the second supervisor.
- Supervisor3ID (bigint): Identifier of the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Name (varchar): Name of the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the third supervisor.
- Supervisor3Phone (varchar): Phone number of the third supervisor.
- Supervisor4ID (bigint): Identifier of the fourth supervisor.
- Supervisor4Name (varchar): Name of the fourth supervisor.
- Supervisor4Vehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the fourth supervisor.
- Supervisor4Phone (varchar): Phone number of the fourth supervisor.
- PlannerID (bigint): Identifier of the planner.
- PlannerName (varchar): Name of the planner.
- PlannerVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the planner.
- PlannerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the planner.
- SectionManagerID (bigint): Identifier of the section manager.
- SectionManagerName (varchar): Name of the section manager.
- SectionManagerVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the section manager.
- SectionManagerPhone (varchar): Phone number of the section manager.
- RouteSheetStatus (varchar): Current status of the route sheet.
- AdditonalNotes (varchar): Additional notes related to the route sheet.
- IsActive (bit): Indicates whether the record is active. Always apply IsActive = 1.


# TABLE : cedemo_CorrosionRSAssignedEmployeeDetails  
Description:  
Stores employee-level assignment details for corrosion route sheets, including employee identification, work details, qualifications, and validation status.

## Columns:
- CorrosionRSAssignedEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the assigned employee record.
- CorrosionRouteSheetID (bigint): Identifier linking the employee to a corrosion route sheet.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS identifier of the employee.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar): CECT ITS identifier of the employee.
- EmployeeName (varchar): Full name of the employee.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identifier.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar): Master employee identifier in CECT system.
- EmployeeVehicle (varchar): Vehicle assigned to the employee.
- WorkLocation (varchar): Location where the employee is assigned to work.
- Borough (varchar): Borough of the work location.
- WorkDescriptionID (bigint): Identifier of the work description.
- WorkDescription (varchar): Description of the assigned work.
- EmployeeDISAPool (varchar): Indicates whether the employee belongs to the DISA pool.
- Qualified (varchar): Indicates whether the employee is qualified (Yes/No).
- MissingCoveredTasks (varchar): Tasks not covered under employee qualifications.
- QualificationValidatedDate (datetime2): Date when qualification was validated.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the record is deleted. Always consider IsDeleted = 0.
- TitleName (varchar): Title of the employee.
- Notes (varchar): Additional notes.


# TABLE : cedemo_CorrosionRSLeaveEmployeeDetails  
Description:  
Stores details of employees on leave for corrosion route sheets, including absence reasons and employee identification.

## Columns:
- CorrosionRSLeaveEmployeeDetailsID (bigint): Unique identifier for the leave employee record.
- CorrosionRouteSheetID (bigint): Identifier linking the leave record to a corrosion route sheet.
- ITSID (varchar): ITS identifier of the employee.
- ITSIDCECT (varchar): CECT ITS identifier of the employee.
- EmployeeName (varchar): Name of the employee.
- EmployeeID (varchar): Employee identifier.
- EmployeeMasterIDCECT (varchar): Master employee identifier in CECT system.
- ReasonForAbsence (varchar): Reason for absence.
- Comments (varchar): Additional comments.
- IsDeleted (bit): Indicates whether the record is deleted. Always consider IsDeleted = 0.


# RELATIONSHIPS:
cedemo_CorrosionRouteSheetHeader.CorrosionRouteSheetID = cedemo_CorrosionRSAssignedEmployeeDetails.CorrosionRouteSheetID  
cedemo_CorrosionRouteSheetHeader.CorrosionRouteSheetID = cedemo_CorrosionRSLeaveEmployeeDetails.CorrosionRouteSheetID  


# FILTER CONDITIONS (ALWAYS APPLY):
- cedemo_CorrosionRouteSheetHeader.IsActive = 1  
- cedemo_CorrosionRSAssignedEmployeeDetails.IsDeleted = 0  
- cedemo_CorrosionRSLeaveEmployeeDetails.IsDeleted = 0
